Apollo 5

Apollo series in one of the famous rocket launch series during the cold war b/w russia(soviets) and USA.

Unmanned Mission

Apollo 5 was an unmanned mission. Its primary goal was to test the Lunar Module in space without risking a human crew.

Lunar Module Testing

This mission provided critical data on the performance of the Lunar Module's ascent and descent propulsion systems.

Earth Orbit Operations

All operations occurred in Earth orbit, simulating key maneuvers planned for future lunar landings.

Successful Engine Burns

The ascent and descent engines were fired multiple times, demonstrating their reliability.
